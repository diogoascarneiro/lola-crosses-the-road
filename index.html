LOLA CROSSES THE ROAD

HOW TO PLAY

  • Your goal is to get Lola back to her house on the other side of the map as fast as possible!
  • Press the arrow keys to move.
  • Lola can only cross the road on crosswalks.
  • Watch out for cars! It's a game over if Lola is hit by one.
  • People will block your way and push you back, so try to avoid them.
